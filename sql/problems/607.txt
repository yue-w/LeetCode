#### Solution 2: right join
SELECT s.name
FROM Company c 
    LEFT JOIN Orders o 
        ON o.com_id = c.com_id AND c.name = "RED"
    RIGHT JOIN SalesPerson s
        ON o.sales_id = s.sales_id
WHERE o.com_id IS NULL
